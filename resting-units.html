Kepler Club's Sleeping Units

For our guests’ comfort, Kepler Club masterfully crafted and manufactured sleeping units with great attention to detail so that our guests can relax or get some sleep, or spend the night before their flights.

Each sleeping unit, also called a “Kep” is uniquely designed to meet the needs of passengers who are going through a tiring flight schedule. Our sleeping units are equipped with sound insulation systems, custom-made ergonomic beds, smart TVs with the capability to watch Netflix and YouTube, a universal charging station, an alarm clock, a negative-pressure air-conditioning system, a tablet computer to track your flight, contact the reception or extend your stay, and a worktable to allow our guests to work while they’re in their beds. Free high speed wifi is also provided for all our guests during their time at Kepler Club.

Kepler Club provides the only sleeping rooms inside Sabiha Gokcen Airport, and other accommodation facilities such as showers, locker rooms and a seating area.

Flying is tiring and stressful, especially while traveling long distances. Sleeping rooms offered by Kepler Club at Sabiha Gokcen are the perfect offer to wind down, take a warm shower and relax for some time before catching your departure flight.
