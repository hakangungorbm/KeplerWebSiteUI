Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check-in and check-out ?

You can check-in and check-out using our self-service kiosks or through our 24-hour reception desk.

What happens if I do not check-out?

It is mandatory for our guests to check-out before they leave Kepler. Guests who do not check-out before leaving our space will be charged for a full day’s price of 108EUR including all taxes.

What happens if I check-out later than my booking timeframe?

Our guests need to notify us through their personal tablet computers or in-person, if they would like to leave after their booking time ends. Guests who check-out late without notifying us are charged 12EUR plus tax per hour during check-out.

How are the orders delivered inside Kepler?

You can do shopping at KeplerShop or at the Duty Free using our tablet computers located next to your bed! The orders are delivered to your cabin by our friendly robot “Yohan”.

Is food served inside Kepler?

No food is served inside Kepler. However, our guests can enjoy free tea and coffee service in our terrace seating salon area.

Can I bring my child who is below 7 years old?

Unfortunately, in order to provide comfortable resting conditions for all our guests, Kepler does not accept children below 7 years old into its sleeping cabin and salon areas. However, our guests can use our family rooms with their children in order to meet their accommodation needs. Please email us at sawairport@keplerclub.com in order to find out if there’s availability for your desired time and to make a reservation.
