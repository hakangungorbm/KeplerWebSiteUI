What We Offer

Self Service Check-In/Out

You can check-in / check-out easily using our self-service kiosks in the entrance area.

Resting Areas with Keps

All features of Kepler are designed to help you relax 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Get some sleep or watch Netflix under the blankets.

Refreshing Points

Showers are available 24 hours a day. Necessary equipments such as shampoo, conditioner, body lotion and towels are provided.

Internet

Free wifi & coffee are provided for all our guests.

Large Seating Area

Comfortable seating area overlooking the whole terminal with printing / scanning capabilities will be waiting for you.

Technology

You will get to experience the first technological resting concept in the world, with delivery robots, personal tablet computers, SmartTVs, electronic smart toilets, self-service kiosks and other automated systems.

Shopping

You can order items such as a bottle of water, face mask and ear plugs using the tablet computer next to your bed. Your orders will be delivered by the special delivery robot of Kepler!
